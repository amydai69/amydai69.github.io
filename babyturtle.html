Baby Turtle

A baby turtle can survive on simple food like dried pellets,
but if you really want him to thrive, you should always aim
for a healthy mix that allows for seasonal availability as well.

Good Foods:

  • Cucumber
  • Earthworms
  • Grasshoppers
